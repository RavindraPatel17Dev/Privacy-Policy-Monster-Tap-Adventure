Advertising

Monster Tap Adventure uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google may collect advertising identifiers and anonymous usage information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads according to your device settings and applicable laws.

Game Data

Your game progress is stored securely on your own device. We do not upload or store your saved game data on our servers.

  • High Scores
  • Unlocked Monsters
  • Collected Coins
  • Achievements
  • Game Settings
  • Sound & Music Preferences
Your game progress remains on your device unless you choose to back it up using your device manufacturer's cloud backup services.

Monster Tap Adventure is intended to be enjoyable for players of all ages.

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through this application, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.
